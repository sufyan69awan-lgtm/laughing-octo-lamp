Chapter 1

So the story starts from a random reel and then our Misa and her guy Sooofiyan met so, they become besties in a few days and although the feelings were confessed later they were there from the first day. Her Sufyan still remembers how careless, dummy and besharam Misa was in the start, but little did he know it was just the beginning of a beautiful love story. To him, she was more than a friend but being honest it felt hard and all he saw was his heart being broken down once again because of her father not agreeing to us He thought highly of her, so obviously his inferiority complex kicked in, which made him think that leading further to the road will only hurt his lover which he didn't want, but at the same time he didn’t have the courage to go back to her and say that he will fix all the things because he was scared to hurt her. But he already knew he had already hurt her in a way when he got scared off from the Margarette thing and hid fear of not being able to meet her father's conditions because yeah she did say it will be up to him to convince him. If he can't convince him then they gotta let go of it, let go of her. So he did what he does best, he ran away knowing it hurt both of them.

Chapter 2

Tried coming back but ran away from fear every time I came to her door to apologize and to tell how much he wants you. I was happy, then we shared everything, like all the things, about how we felt about each other, how much we liked each other, and although the fears were still there we just chose to risk it all. Chapter 3

So hence the story begins, not your everyday love story, more like two people finding peace in taking care of each other rather than loving because they think they are best for each other or they can’t find someone better but solely because they are just dumb enough to live for their beloved one. Yeah, someone they love. Dumb, aren't they? How can someone live their whole life for someone? That’s why the story is different, that’s what I meant by it. So yeah, they started knowing each other more and more. The boy's beloved one started healing and the boy was happy to be part of her healing arc. Ups and downs are part of life, aren't they? So they began to heal, began to understand each other. A thought crossed the boy's mind since both of them +were fixated upon trying not to hurt each other and then the concept of 2 bodies one soul crossed his mind. He saw that even those who love fight, even those who respect each other fight, they hurt each other. But he also thought how can they hold malice for each other when they show love and affection on their faces? He tried to go on with the idea of 2 bodies one soul, solved it a bit and the idea was loving someone selflessly, which they were already doing. It was thinking of the other person as their equal and how they are as much as important as their own self. The ideology didn’t have just words backing it up that she is his equal, it was more of a pursuit of a spiritual thing, treating them as much as you can, sometimes even more than themselves, and hence the theory worked, alhamdulillah. It shall work as long as they don’t hide what’s in their heart, they will say it to their faces. His girl was always straightforward and he loved that fact that she trusted him so much that she just said everything. But the boy had to work on that thing since always he tried to convey his feelings, he was kind of looked at as a toxic guy, being called possessive, controlling etc. But she heard him, never judged him, which made him finally feel like there is someone who is exactly like him. And yeah, the boy is still trying to open up further since you are a home for him. By him saying she is a part of him, they aren’t just empty words. He believes that anything is possible, just like they say "You have power over your mind, not outside events. Realize this and you will find strength." So yeah, it's possible to love someone more than yourself, but it all happens with some effort. To love is a choice and we love you our other half.
